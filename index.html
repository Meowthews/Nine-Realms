A Xianxia Progression Mod For Elin

The Dao Does Not Forgive Hesitation.

Nine Realms: Path of the Dao brings long-form cultivation progression into Elin. Open the first gate, choose your path, temper your body or refine your qi, and push upward through mortal limits toward the immortal realms.

This is not a sprint system. Cultivation drains stamina and mana. Advancement stalls at the gate until you risk a breakthrough. Failure hurts. Success changes everything.

Current Direction Body and Qi cultivation are live, with rogue cultivation support, shared immortal realms, breakthrough risk, and a dedicated in-game cultivation menu.

What The Mod Adds

A framework-first cultivation experience built around pacing, path identity, resource pressure, and dangerous realm progression.

Initiation

Open The First Gate

Take the Cultivation Initiate feat to unlock cultivation itself, then commit to Body, Qi, or remain a rogue wanderer for a slower and broader ascent.

Progression

Nine Mortal Realms, Nine Immortal Realms

Body and Qi paths each climb through nine distinct major realms before converging into nine shared immortal realms that raise the ceiling to truly absurd heights.

Risk

Breakthroughs Matter

Realm progress stops at the gate. If you want the next realm, you must challenge the bottleneck and accept the chance of lightning backlash, stat loss pressure, and collapse.

Three Ways To Walk

Each route carries a different texture. Formal paths gain stronger efficiencies and breakthroughs, while rogues trade certainty for breadth.

Body

Body Cultivator

Skin to marrow, flesh to iron. Body cultivation leans into direct physical supremacy.

  • Primary growth: STR, DEX, PV, SPD
  • Resource reward focus: max HP and stamina
  • Realms culminate in Indestructible Body before immortality
Qi

Qi Practitioner

Gather breath, condense spirit, establish foundation, and climb through classic xianxia milestones.

  • Primary growth: CHA, INT, WIL, DV
  • Resource reward focus: max HP and mana
  • Realms culminate in Tribulation Transcendence before immortality
Rogue

Rogue Cultivator

No sect. No scripture. No formal gate. A rogue cultivator improves more slowly, but across a broader set of attributes.

  • Can cultivate without choosing Body or Qi
  • Cannot formally break through
  • Can still advance up to the current shared cap
Orientation

Designed For The Long Road

Power in Nine Realms arrives the way it should: slowly, painfully, and then all at once. Lower realms are meant to take time. Formal paths become more efficient at higher tiers. Breakthrough success drops as the heavens demand more from you.
