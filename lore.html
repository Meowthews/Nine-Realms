Documented Ability

Cultivate

To cultivate is to sit before heaven and earth, draw breath through the mortal shell, and force spirit, body, and intent into alignment. It is not a burst of power. It is a deliberate act of ascent.

Cultivate is the core long-action ability unlocked through Cultivation Initiate. It is the primary method used to gain cultivation progress and push toward the next realm.

Mechanics

Cost And Flow

  • Cultivation is a long action that spends stamina and mana every tick.
  • If stamina or mana drops too low, cultivation stops automatically.
  • Rogue cultivators always pay 3 stamina and 3 mana per cultivation tick.
  • Formal cultivators begin at the same cost, then improve to 2 and eventually 1 at higher tiers.
Progress

What It Builds Toward

  • Each cultivation tick advances progress toward the current realm's cap.
  • Progress scales with Meditation, current realm, and path-relevant stats.
  • Body cultivation leans on STR, DEX, and PV. Qi cultivation leans on CHA, INT, WIL, and DV.
  • When progress reaches the gate, advancement pauses until a breakthrough is attempted.
Neutral

Profound Qi Healing

A measured circulation of inward qi turns breath into mending force, drawing on inner reserves to quiet pain and knit flesh. It is not violent. It is the patient answer of a cultivator who refuses to fall.

Profound Qi Healing is a neutral healing art housed on the Techniques page of the cultivation menu. It converts qi into recovery and grows more efficient as formal cultivators rise through higher realms.

Function

How It Works

  • It restores health every tick by draining mana, representing qi expenditure.
  • The technique is toggled within the cultivation menu.
  • Rogue or low-realm formal cultivators heal 2 HP per tick at a cost of 3 MP.
  • At realm 5 and above, it improves to 3 HP for 2 MP. At realm 9 and above, it improves again to 4 HP for 1 MP.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • This is a neutral healing technique centered on recovery rather than offense.
  • It gives cultivators a steady way to turn inner reserves into survivability.
  • Its efficiency deepens most sharply for formal paths as realms rise.
Body Refiner

Iron Skin

Body qi is pressed outward until the skin takes on the feel of forged hide. Flesh grows dense, blows glance aside more easily, and movement loses some of its softness.

Iron Skin is the first body refiner technique. It is a body-path toggle that hardens the cultivator for direct punishment, trading nimbleness for a tougher frame.

Function

How It Works

  • Iron Skin is a toggle technique with no per-tick stamina or mana cost.
  • At lower realms it grants +2 PV and -3 DV.
  • At realm 5 and above it becomes +3 PV and -2 DV.
  • At realm 9 and above it becomes +4 PV and -1 DV.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It is a defensive body art built to blunt direct punishment.
  • The cost is not resource drain, but reduced evasiveness while it remains active.
  • As the body cultivator climbs higher realms, the protection improves and the drawback softens.
Qi Practitioner

Flowing Meridian

Qi is threaded through the meridians until the body feels wind-borne and difficult to pin down. The price is that flesh and bone carry less certainty when a strike lands cleanly.

Flowing Meridian is the first Qi Practitioner technique. It is a qi-path toggle that sharpens evasiveness and lightness while making the cultivator less suited to blunt impact.

Function

How It Works

  • Flowing Meridian is a toggle technique with no per-tick stamina or mana cost.
  • At lower realms it grants +2 DV and -3 PV.
  • At realm 5 and above it becomes +3 DV and -2 PV.
  • At realm 9 and above it becomes +4 DV and -1 PV.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It is an evasive qi art built around lightness, flow, and repositioning.
  • The cost is not resource drain, but reduced physical protection while it remains active.
  • As realms rise, the evasive bonus grows and the protection penalty eases.
