Documented Ability

Cultivate

To cultivate is to sit before heaven and earth, draw breath through the mortal shell, and force spirit, body, and intent into alignment. It is not a burst of power. It is a deliberate act of ascent.

Cultivate is the core long-action ability unlocked through Cultivation Initiate. It is the primary method used to gain cultivation progress and push toward the next realm.

Function

How It Works

  • Each cultivation tick spends stamina and MP: 3/3 by default, 2/2 at realm 5+, and 1/1 at realm 9+. Still Breath Sutra lowers each cost by 1, to a minimum of 1.
  • Formal progress per tick is max(1, 1 + realm / 2 + Meditation / 150 + path stat bonus), then cultivation speed and Still Breath modifiers apply.
  • Rogue progress per tick is max(1, 1 + Meditation / 250 + all rogue cultivation attributes / 1200).
  • Realm gates are every 1000 progress up to 18000; formal paths pause at the next gate - 1 until breakthrough, while rogues can progress to the cap but cannot break through.
Ability Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • Formal stat gain chance is clamp(1 + realm + progress gained / 2 + Meditation / 100, 2%, 20%). Shared immortal realms add immortal rank and clamp to 25%.
  • Rogue stat gain chance is clamp(1 + progress / 2000, 1%, 5%) and can roll STR, END, DEX, PER, LER, WIL, MAG, or CHA.
  • Body cultivation rolls STR, END, DEX, PER, WIL, or PV. Qi cultivation rolls CHA, WIL, MAG, PER, LER, Meditation, or DV.
  • Passive realm bonuses are Body: STR/END/DEX/PER/WIL +realm and PV +(realm * 2); Qi: CHA/PER/LER/WIL/MAG +realm and DV +(realm * 2).
  • Body breakthrough success grants STR/END/DEX/PER/WIL +(2 + new realm), PV +(1 + new realm / 2), max HP +(10 + (new realm - 1) * 5), and max stamina by the same amount.
  • Qi breakthrough success grants CHA/PER/LER/WIL/MAG +(2 + new realm), Meditation and DV +(1 + new realm / 2), max HP +(10 + (new realm - 1) * 5), and max MP by the same amount.
  • Shared immortal breakthrough success grants all cultivation attributes +(1 + immortal rank), PV/DV/Meditation +max(1, immortal rank / 2), and max HP/MP/stamina +(10 + (immortal rank - 1) * 10).
Documented Ability

Open Minor Meridian

A minor meridian is not opened gently. The cultivator drives qi and blood into a narrow gate until it yields, forcing a sudden surge of progress. If the pulse twists, the same pressure bites back through the channels.

Open Minor Meridian is a risky cultivation accelerator: spend MP and stamina for immediate progress, with a small chance of backlash.

Function

How It Works

  • Costs 8 + realm * 2 stamina and the same amount of MP.
  • Grants 25 + realm * 5 + Meditation / 20 cultivation progress immediately.
  • Backlash chance is min(18, 10 + realm)%; backlash deals MaxHP * min(18, 5 + realm * 2) / 100 and drains max(2, realm * 2) stamina and MP.
Ability Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It is a risky accelerator for cultivators willing to force the gate.
  • The reward is speed; the price is resource pressure and possible injury.
  • It fits desperate pushes before a breakthrough or aggressive realm chasing.
Neutral

Profound Qi Healing

A measured circulation of inward qi turns breath into mending force, drawing on inner reserves to quiet pain and knit flesh. It is not violent. It is the patient answer of a cultivator who refuses to fall.

Profound Qi Healing is a neutral healing art housed on the Techniques page of the cultivation menu. It converts qi into recovery and grows more efficient as formal cultivators rise through higher realms.

Function

How It Works

  • It restores health every tick by draining mana, representing qi expenditure.
  • The technique is toggled within the cultivation menu.
  • Rogue or low-realm formal cultivators heal 2 HP per tick at a cost of 3 MP.
  • At realm 5 and above, it improves to 3 HP for 2 MP. At realm 9 and above, it improves again to 4 HP for 1 MP.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • This is a neutral healing technique centered on recovery rather than offense.
  • It gives cultivators a steady way to turn inner reserves into survivability.
  • Its efficiency deepens most sharply for formal paths as realms rise.
Neutral

Still Breath Sutra

The sutra teaches a cultivator to make each breath narrow, quiet, and almost unmoving. Qi no longer rushes like a river; it gathers like winter mist, conserving the body's fire even as the pace of ascent becomes more measured.

Still Breath Sutra is a neutral passive for patient cultivation. It reduces the stamina and MP cost of cultivation by 1, but slightly lowers cultivation progress gained per tick.

Function

How It Works

  • Reduces cultivation stamina cost by 1.
  • Reduces cultivation MP cost by 1.
  • Progress becomes 80% before realm 5, 85% at realm 5+, and 90% at realm 9+.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • Best suited to long, low-risk cultivation sessions.
  • It favors endurance and steadiness over rapid realm pressure.
  • Its flavor is restraint: wasting less qi, forcing less heaven.
Neutral

Hidden Vessel Art

Old manuals speak of vessels beneath the known meridians, quiet channels that do not answer ordinary breathing methods. This art coaxes them open, giving the cultivator deeper reserves without demanding allegiance to body or qi alone.

Hidden Vessel Art is a neutral passive that increases max MP and max stamina by a small amount that scales with realm.

Function

How It Works

  • Max MP increases by 4 + realm * 2.
  • Max stamina increases by 4 + realm * 2.
  • Examples: realm 1 gives +6, realm 5 gives +14, and realm 9 gives +22.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It supports both formal paths without pushing either identity too hard.
  • The art makes longer technique chains and longer cultivation sessions easier to sustain.
  • Its gains are quiet, but they touch the two reserves every cultivator spends.
Body Refiner

Iron Skin

Body qi is pressed outward until the skin takes on the feel of forged hide. Flesh grows dense, blows glance aside more easily, and movement loses some of its softness.

Iron Skin is the first body refiner technique. It is a body-path toggle that hardens the cultivator for direct punishment, trading nimbleness for a tougher frame.

Function

How It Works

  • Iron Skin is a toggle technique with no per-tick stamina or mana cost.
  • At lower realms it grants +2 PV and -3 DV.
  • At realm 5 and above it becomes +3 PV and -2 DV.
  • At realm 9 and above it becomes +4 PV and -1 DV.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It is a defensive body art built to blunt direct punishment.
  • The cost is not resource drain, but reduced evasiveness while it remains active.
  • As the body cultivator climbs higher realms, the protection improves and the drawback softens.
Body Refiner

Bone Tempering Art

The art grinds qi into the skeleton until every bone remembers pressure. Marrow grows hot, joints settle like locked iron, and the cultivator becomes harder to break even when grace is sacrificed.

Bone Tempering Art is a body passive that grants realm-scaled max HP and PV, with a small DV penalty.

Function

How It Works

  • Max HP increases by 8 + realm * 4.
  • PV is +2 plus realm / 2 before realm 5, +4 at realm 5+, and +5 at realm 9+.
  • DV penalty is -4 before realm 3, -3 at realm 3+, -2 at realm 5+, and -1 at realm 9+.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It is the clearest expression of the body path: endure first, move second.
  • The art rewards higher realms with a sturdier frame and stronger guard.
  • Its drawback keeps the body refiner grounded and heavy.
Body Refiner

Hundred Scars Scripture

Each wound is written into the flesh like a line of scripture. Pain becomes annotation, bruises become doctrine, and a body refiner who survives enough punishment begins to turn suffering into momentum.

Hundred Scars Scripture is a body passive. When the cultivator takes damage, there is a small chance to gain cultivation progress or temporary toughness.

Function

How It Works

  • Drains 1 stamina per tick while active.
  • When taking HP damage, has min(22, 8 + realm)% chance to grant 4 + realm * 2 cultivation progress.
  • Also grants temporary PV + (1 + min(3, realm / 3)) for 20 + realm * 5 ticks.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It turns hardship into advancement without making pain feel free.
  • The best body refiners can use battle itself as a rough cultivation chamber.
  • It rewards survival, not recklessness.
Body Refiner

Blood Furnace

The dantian is stoked like a red furnace and the blood answers. Refined mana is burned down into heat, heat becomes breath, and breath becomes the raw stamina a body refiner needs to keep moving.

Blood Furnace is a body passive that converts mana into stamina for body refiners.

Function

How It Works

  • Before realm 5, spends 3 MP per tick to restore 1 stamina.
  • At realm 5+, spends 2 MP per tick to restore 2 stamina.
  • At realm 9+, spends 1 MP per tick to restore 3 stamina; turns off if MP is insufficient.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It gives mana a body-path purpose without making the refiner feel like a spellcaster.
  • The technique frames inner energy as fuel for flesh, blood, and motion.
  • It is practical, brutal, and very readable as body refinement.
Qi Practitioner

Flowing Meridian

Qi is threaded through the meridians until the body feels wind-borne and difficult to pin down. The price is that flesh and bone carry less certainty when a strike lands cleanly.

Flowing Meridian is the first Qi Practitioner technique. It is a qi-path toggle that sharpens evasiveness and lightness while making the cultivator less suited to blunt impact.

Function

How It Works

  • Flowing Meridian is a toggle technique with no per-tick stamina or mana cost.
  • At lower realms it grants +2 DV and -3 PV.
  • At realm 5 and above it becomes +3 DV and -2 PV.
  • At realm 9 and above it becomes +4 DV and -1 PV.
Technique Nature

Role In Cultivation

  • It is an evasive qi art built around lightness, flow, and repositioning.
  • The cost is not resource drain, but reduced physical protection while it remains active.
  • As realms rise, the evasive bonus grows and the protection penalty eases.
