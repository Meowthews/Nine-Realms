Quick Start + Mechanical Reference

Step Into Cultivation Without Guesswork.

This page covers how to begin, what each path does, how progress and stages work, and exactly what the breakthrough system is asking from you.

Quick Start Realms & Stages Core Systems Breakthroughs
Journal Access

Once Cultivation Initiate is awakened, the cultivation menu can be opened through the Nine Realms entry in the in-game player journal.

Quick Start

1. Buy Cultivation Initiate

Spend 2 feat points on Cultivation Initiate. This unlocks the ability to cultivate and the cultivation menu.

2. Choose Your Direction

Body Cultivator and Qi Practitioner each cost 2 feat points, require Cultivation Initiate, and are mutually exclusive. If you choose neither, you remain a Rogue Cultivator.

3. Start Cultivating

Cultivation is a long action that spends stamina and mana each tick. When either pool is too low, cultivation stops automatically.

4. Reach The Gate

Progress rises toward the next realm threshold. Formal paths stop at the bottleneck one point before the next major realm.

5. Attempt Breakthrough

When you are standing at the gate, use the cultivation menu to attempt a breakthrough. Success advances you. Failure triggers backlash.

At A Glance

  • Rogue cultivators can keep training, but they cannot formally break through.
  • Body cultivation focuses on STR, END, DEX, PER, WIL, and PV.
  • Qi cultivation focuses on CHA, PER, LER, WIL, MAG, DV, and Meditation.
  • Formal cultivators become more efficient as their realms rise.
  • Shared immortal realms unlock after the ninth Body or Qi realm.
  • The cultivation menu is reached through the in-game player journal.

Realms & Stages

Every major realm spans 1000 cultivation progress. Formal paths move through four internal stages across that span: Early, Middle, Late, and Peak. At the end of the span, progression pauses at the gate until the breakthrough succeeds.

Realm structure Thresholds: 1000, 2000, 3000 ... up to 18000 Formal paths stop at next threshold - 1 until breakthrough Stages divide each 1000-point realm into Early / Middle / Late / Peak

Body Realms

Skin Tempering, Muscle Forging, Tendon Strengthening, Bone Tempering, Marrow Cleansing, Blood Refinement, Organ Refinement, Golden Body, Indestructible Body.

Qi Realms

Qi Gathering, Qi Condensation, Foundation Establishment, Core Formation, Nascent Soul, Soul Transformation, Void Refinement, Body Integration, Tribulation Transcendence.

True Immortal, Earth Immortal, Heavenly Immortal, Profound Immortal, Golden Immortal, Taiyi Immortal, Immortal King, Immortal Emperor, Dao Ancestor.

Core Systems

Cultivation Cost

Rogue cultivators always pay 3 stamina and 3 mana per cultivation tick. Formal cultivators also start at 3 and improve to 2 at higher mid-realms, then 1 once they reach the top tiers.

Progress Per Tick

Progress scales from meditation, your current realm, and path-relevant stats. Body uses STR, END, DEX, PER, WIL, and PV. Qi uses CHA, PER, LER, WIL, MAG, and DV.

Repeated Training Gains

Every cultivation session can permanently improve attributes. Formal paths get sharper odds and more focused growth as their realm rises. Rogues grow broader, slower, and less reliably.

Progress Formula Summary

Rogue progress/tick = 1 + Meditation / 250 + rogue attribute total / 1200 Formal progress/tick = 1 + realm / 2 + Meditation / 150 + path stat bonus Body path bonus = (STR + END + DEX + PER + WIL) / 625 + PV / 100, capped at +12 Qi path bonus = (CHA + PER + LER + WIL + MAG) / 625 + DV / 125, capped at +12

Stat Gain Summary

Rogue gain chance = 1 + progress / 2000, clamped to 1% - 5% Formal gain chance = 1 + realm + progress gained this tick / 2 + Meditation / 100 Clamped to 2% - 20% Shared immortal gain chance gains extra rank scaling and is clamped to 2% - 25%

Breakthrough System

Breakthroughs are the wall that turns cultivation from background grind into a real gamble. Before the gate, success chance is zero. At the gate, your odds are calculated from your current realm plus the stats that fit your path. Higher realms become harder on purpose.

Success Chance

If not at the gate: 0% If Rogue Cultivator: 0% At the gate: 28 - current realm * 2 + relevant stat bonus Final result is clamped between 3% and 45% Relevant stat bonus is capped at +8%

Breakthrough Cost

Stamina cost = max(10, current cultivation stamina cost * 3) Mana cost = max(10, current cultivation mana cost * 3) Failure causes: Lightning damage Extra stamina loss Extra mana loss Cultivation Backlash

Failure Consequences

On failure, lightning tears through the cultivator's body. Damage scales from your maximum HP and current realm. The backlash also drains extra stamina and mana, then applies Cultivation Backlash on top.

Failure math Damage = MaxHP * min(35, 6 + realm * 3) / 100 Extra stamina loss = max(5, realm * 3) Extra mana loss = max(5, realm * 3)

Why Formal Paths Overtake Rogues

Rogues can build wide attribute spreads and keep training without committing. But the moment formal cultivators start clearing realms and especially enter the shared immortal ladder, they begin stacking breakthrough rewards, passive realm bonuses, and better cultivation efficiency on top of every session.
